Latitude vs. Max Temperature



Observations


The highest temperature was recorded in Santiago del Estero, Argentina at 99.73˚F, while the lowest temperature recorded was Ust-Nera, Russia at -44.73˚F. We see smaller latitiudes getting higher temperatures as they are closer to the equator. The opposite goes for bigger latitudes.
