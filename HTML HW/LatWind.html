Latitude vs. Wind Speeds



Observations


The highest wind speed recorded was 47.2 mph in Punta Arenas, Chile. It seems the further you get from the equator, the higher the wind speeds. Could this be due to the lower humidity levels in the air, presenting less wind resistance?
